Barbaros

The Story

Barbaros: Sword of the Meditarranean is a Turkish historical fiction television
series created by ES Film, starring Engin Altan Düzyatan and Ulaş Tuna Astepe
in the lead roles. The series will depict the life of Barbaros Hayreddin Pasha,
a "Kapudan Pasha" of the Ottoman Empire.
